1️⃣ Ska + infinitive

Form: ska + infinitive

Use:

Use case	Example	English
Plan / intention	Jag ska ringa dig imorgon.	“I am going to call you tomorrow.”
Promise / decision made	Vi ska hjälpa dig.	“We will help you.”
Strong personal intention	Han ska sluta röka.	“He is going to quit smoking.”

Key idea: Usually emphasizes plan, intention, or decision, often personal.

2️⃣ Kommer att + infinitive

Form: kommer att + infinitive

Use:

Use case	Example	English
Prediction	Det kommer att regna i eftermiddag.	“It will rain this afternoon.”
Scheduled future events (objective)	Biblioteket kommer att öppna klockan 10.	“The library will open at 10.”
Future facts / expectations	Staden kommer att bygga nya cykelbanor.	“The city will build new bike lanes.”

Key idea: Often neutral, factual, or predictive, not emphasizing personal intention.

3️⃣ Key differences
Feature	ska + infinitive	kommer att + infinitive
Focus	personal plan, decision	prediction, scheduled events
Tone	subjective / intentional	neutral / objective
Common with weather, news, schedules	❌	✅
